(V) ILLUSTRATIVE CASES

 

 

These cases deal with the basic personal tax credit, the employee tax credit and the standard rate band.  Discretionary tax reliefs such as mortgage interest relief, or relief on rent paid, or charges such as benefits in kind are not taken into account. However, the gain from child benefit and the first full year of the Early Childcare Supplement are also included. Some of the figures in the following examples are rounded to the nearest euro.
